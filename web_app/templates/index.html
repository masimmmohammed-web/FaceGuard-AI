How It Works

Current live pipeline used by this model

Camera Stream

Browser captures frames and sends lightweight JPEGs to the server for real-time analysis (rate-limited and auto-aborted for stability).

Face Detection

Server detects face ROI and landmarks. The UI only proceeds after a few consecutive positive detections to avoid flicker.

Eye Tracking + Landmarks

Eyes, bboxes, and 6-point eye landmarks are extracted. Pupil centers are estimated and rendered on the overlay for visual feedback.

Blink Verification (EAR/mEAR)

EAR and pose‑normalized mEAR are computed with EMA smoothing. A brief consecutive‑frame threshold confirms a blink for liveness.

Anti‑Spoofing

Texture, sharpness and motion signals estimate liveness risk. Results populate the Anti‑Spoofing tile in the live report.

Deepfake Scoring

On blink success, frames are scored by the deepfake engine. Whole-frame and face-crop probabilities are combined into overall risk.

Final Assessment

Overall risk → AUTHENTIC / SUSPICIOUS / DEEPFAKE. Status badges and results are updated live in the dashboard.
